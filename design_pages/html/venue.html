DESCRIPTION

Residents of Vizianagaram have a prime destination to host their grand events. S Convention Hall at Vizianagaram is the destination to be for all the celebrations to come. This impeccable venue has everything under its hat to turn any occasion into a showstopper. This air conditioned hall with a seating capacity of 200-800 is ideal for events like weddings, get together parties, corporate events, political party meetings, seminars, presentations and many more of these orders. A separate dining hall of an accommodating capacity of 400 is another plus point. Ample parking space, in house florists/decorators, catering facility are the other amenities offered at the venue. There are two complimentary rooms at the service of the hosts. One can either choose the outside caterers or avail the in house kitchen facility. Elegance, grandeur and royalty are now synonymous with S Convention Hall. Any event celebrated here shall be an epic for the times to come.
